Overview

Purpose

Have you ever been worried about the safety of you and your children? Have you ever wondered if there was someplace you all could go to learn how to defend yourself while keeping the family together? If so my website is the perfect one for you. I designed this website so that family’s could train and grow stronger together and gain confidence in the real world. As a black belt I know the struggles of first starting off in a new style. So my website will be friendly to all ranks, no matter if this is your first lesson with us, or your hundredth. If the style we practice is not for you, don’t worry my website can recommend you to many others until you find a style you love

Audience

The intended audience for this website is for young adult families, with children struggling with making friends and or defending themselves. While adults are more than welcome into the Dojo, are main focus is on the children and helping them grow confidence and the strength to defend themselves. So anyone that is considered a loner or a recluse will be welcome with open arms in this place.

Home page

People will come to the site to learn how to properly defend themselves when faced with dangerous situations. They will also come to make friendships with people of all ages and backgrounds. They will come not only to strengthen their body, but also their mind as they become used to making lightning-fast decisions. People will work on becoming the very best versions of themselves that they can be through hard work and determination. As long as they give their full effort I will promise them results.

If they do I will offer the first lesson free of charge to get the parents and the children to understand how my Dojo will run. They will be able to see the fact that while I will not fully separate the adults from the kids, But they will be able to fully understand the results that their children will be able to perform if they stick to being a student under me. History

The style of Martial Art I will be teaching will be the ATA style of Taekwondo. This style of Taekwondo was founded in the 1969 in Omaha, Nebraska by Haeng Ung Lee of South Korea. Who was inspired by a service man he taught to open the first school in 1969. One of the ways that the ATA looks at the growth of their students is like viewing the birth and life of a pine tree. Starting from White all the way to the red belt shows the first stages of life. From growing and learning how to survive and even thrive as the Pine tree keeps growing taller and taller. White Belt Orange Belt Yellow Belt Camouflage Belt Green Belt Purple Belt Blue Belt Brown Belt Red Belt From Black belt and beyond the tree Has reached its true maturity although it can still grow, instead now its main focus will be to help nurture other saplings and help them grow. 1st Degree Black Belt Recommended 1st Degree Black Belt 2nd Degree Black Belt 3rd Degree Black Belt 4th Degree Black Belt 5th Degree Black Belt 6th Degree Black Belt 7th Degree Black Belt 8th Degree Black Belt 9th Degree Black Belt As you can see once you finally attain your Black Belt, the journey is not over. Instead your journey is truly beginning to take off. If you stick to this there is no telling how far you will be able to go. I hope to be able to help watch your journey and help you grow to the very best person that you can be. •
